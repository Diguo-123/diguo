About Diguo

we are a small organisation that helps disadvantaged people , by donating what we can to those in need. as most of people cannot afford basic needs , our goal is to help people access basic needs and reduce poverty as much as we can.

you can help us by donating school stationery , food , clothes , pads or by funding so we can use them to help people who cannot afford them.

How to help us

this is how to help by donating any of the following

Food

Donating food to worthy people or organizations helps counter poverty, hunger and at the same time improve harmony friendliness , and trust among residents

clothing

when you donate clothes you help those in need ,to protect them against harsh environmental conditions and help them in improving their sanitation and getting helthier

school stationery

stationery is the basic requirement for learning withought it students are incapable of writing down the notes taking tests and studying effectively

the small charity that comes from the heart is better than the great charity that comes from the head.
wealth is not to feed our egos but to feed the hungry and to help people help themselves.
